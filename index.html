OUR GAMES

TIC-TAC: Twelve o'clock

Release Date: Coming soon
TIC-TAC: Twelve o'clock is a single adventure–puzzle game with a storyline that runs through survival horror in the Bluebird Middle school. You must spend several nights in an abandoned building, trying to find out whats going on, and protect your life. TIC-TAC: Twelve o'clock Game is inspired by cult horrors of the early 90s. You have to help a group of schoolchildren–adventurers to solve the dark mystery of the abandoned school.
  • original plot
  • gloomy atmosphere of isolation
  • dynamic gameplay with hard timings
  • puzzles and random events
  • detailed style of a retro horror
Are you ready to challenge evil when 12 o'clock strikes?
ABOUT

Armature Games

Armature Games is an independent studio focused on atmospheric, story‑driven horror. We craft tight, suspenseful experiences with attention to pacing, sound and environmental storytelling. Our debut project, TIC‑TAC: Twelve o'clock, blends survival elements with puzzle mechanics and a retro‑horror aesthetic.
We take part in festivals and community events and are grateful for the support from players and partners around the world.
